Hovding.

Hövding is a bicycle helmet unlike any other currently on the market. It’s ergonomic, practical, and more importantly, Hovding complies with all the safety requirements, and it’s also fashionable and blends in with what you are wearing.

Learn More

Creative Execution

To increase awareness on the Hovding helmet, because it can save lives of cyclists who refuse to wear hard shell helmets.

Executive Summary

Hövding is an invisible helmet that is worn like a collar around your neck. When it senses shock, it releases a white helmet made from an ultra-strong nylon fabric that wraps around your head for protection, while leaving the field for vision open. The collar device inflates with a gas that is also know as a cold gas inflator, which uses helium and later deflates within 10 seconds after impact.

Purpose

Every year approximately 30,000 people are injured in bicycling accidents, resulting in about 40 deaths in Sweden alone. Many riders choose to not wear a helmet because of fear of looking “stupid” and not wanting to mess up their hair or style.
